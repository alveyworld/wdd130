Prophets of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Joseph Smith

Prophet 1830-1844

Visited by God and Jesus Christ in answer to his prayer to find out which church to join. As the Lord's servant he helped restore the Church of Jesus Christ!

Brigham Young

Prophet 1847-1877

Modern Moses who led the Latter-day Saints across the plains to settle in the west and start building the Salt Lake Temple

John Taylor

Prophet 1880-1887

Saved by a pocket watch during when the mob came to kill Joseph and Hyrum.

Wilford Woodruff

Prophet 1889-1898

Wrote down and documented much of the doing of Joseph Smith and the restoration. Received revelation to end the practice of polygamy.

Lorenzo Snow

Prophet 1898-1901

Taught the people of Saint George that blessing come from obedience to the law of tithing.

Joseph F. Smith

Prophet 1901-1918

Grandson of Hyrum Smith.
